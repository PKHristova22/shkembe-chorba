Educational robots are robots designed specifically for educational purposes. They are used in classrooms to enhance learning
in STEM subjects. These robots engage students through interactive activities, allowing hands-on experiences and programming
opportunities. Educational robots can adapt to individual student needs, providing personalized instruction and feedback.
