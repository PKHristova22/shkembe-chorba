Educational robots are robots designed specifically for educational purposes. They are used in classrooms to enhance learning
in STEM subjects. These robots engage students through interactive activities, allowing hands-on experiences and programming
opportunities.Educational robots can adapt to individual student needs, providing personalized instruction and feedback. By
integrating these robots into the learning environment, students develop important skills for the future while enjoying a dynamic
and interactive learning experience.
